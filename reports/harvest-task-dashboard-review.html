Collections Triage

Choose work here by Accrual Periodicity. Monthly, quarterly, annual, and other scheduled records need reharvesting; irregular records need review. Update the Harvest record with a queue tag and due date, sync it, and rerun Triage to add it to To do.

Dashboard tag cheat sheet

Add these in the Harvest record’s Tags field. Separate multiple tags with a pipe, comma, or semicolon.

queue:pending_harvest
Queue a reharvest.
queue:pending_updates
Queue a Harvest record update.
queue:pending_review
Queue a review.
due:YYYY-MM-DD
Set the due date for queued work; harvest_due:YYYY-MM-DD is also accepted. Without a date, queued work is due today.
review:Ny
Review an irregular record every N years, such as review:2y. Triage highlights upcoming and overdue reviews.
